    • VI. Gameplay

    Mechanics

    What specific choices can the player make and what can they do?

  • The player is able to input a wager amount from their current credits (will begin with a default value)
  • The player is able to "cash out" at any time by pressing a button during the game.
  • The player must decide to risk waiting for the line to grow longer (multiplyer increase) or cashout with their current winnings.
  • Because the amount of time is random, the player could theoretically lose their wager within the first 1 or 2 seconds.
